title: Forest Allies Pledge Moss-Pool Stewardship
summary: Trail groups adopt guidelines to protect sensitive canopy pools.
---
Regional trail associations have adopted stewardship guidelines: avoid pruning limbs supporting epiphytic mats, minimize campfire smoke near canopy pools, and report invasive species. These practical steps reduce stress on Tree Trout microhabitats while supporting broader forest health.
