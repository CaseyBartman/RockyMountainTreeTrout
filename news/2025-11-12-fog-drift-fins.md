title: Prehensile Fin Study Shows Bark-Grip Advantage
summary: Field notes indicate improved bark adherence during vertical ascents.
---
A new study documents micro-hooks along Tree Trout pectoral fins that increase bark adherence by up to 27% on rough-barked spruce. Video analysis shows a rhythmic "grip and glide" pattern enabling efficient ascents to canopy pools after seasonal storms.
